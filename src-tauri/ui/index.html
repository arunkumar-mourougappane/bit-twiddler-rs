Two's Complement

Most modern systems use **Two's Complement** for signed integers. The MSB (Most Significant Bit) acts as the sign bit.

Formula for `n` bits:
Signed: `[-2^(n-1), 2^(n-1) - 1]`
Unsigned: `[0, 2^n - 1]`
